Step 2 · the split

🧭Every item → Analyst guidance
All items (including the triggers) carry forward as intent that shapes the written advice — never as figures.
Execution style · "buy in tranches"
Selection criteria · "low fees, good liquidity"
Open question · "impact of rate hikes?"
⚠️ Anything ambiguous or contradictory is typed needs-clarification — held out of the proposal until you resolve it. And if the client stated target weights, Confirm surfaces a Proposed allocation to Apply.

Step 4 · the guardrail

Guidance shapes the story — never the numbers

Guidance can shape
  • The monitoring watchlist
  • The written commentary & emphasis
  • What the analyst is prompted to weigh
Guidance never changes
  • Allocation, drift & rebalancing figures
  • Suitability checks
  • Any dollar amount — all computed from real holdings

Ad-hoc words become things the copilot can act on and monitor — without ever letting the model invent a number.
