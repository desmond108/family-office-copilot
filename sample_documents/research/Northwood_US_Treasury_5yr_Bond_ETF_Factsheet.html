Investment objective

The fund seeks to track the total return of an index of US Treasury notes with remaining maturities of three to seven years. It is designed as a low-cost, highly liquid core building block for the intermediate-duration portion of a fixed-income allocation.

Why it fits an intermediate-duration sleeve

  • Low cost: a 0.07% TER is among the lowest in the category.
  • Deep liquidity: large AUM and tight spreads support tranche-based buying without meaningful market impact.
  • High quality: pure US Treasury exposure, no credit or FX overlay.
  • Medium duration: a 4.6-year duration sits in the middle of the curve — sensitive to rate moves but less so than long-dated bonds.

Risk considerations

Interest-rate risk is the primary driver: a 1% rise in yields would reduce the fund's price by approximately its duration (~4.6%), before carry. The fund does not hedge inflation directly. Past performance and yields are not a guide to future returns.
