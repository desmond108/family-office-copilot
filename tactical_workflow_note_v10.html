06 / 06

The guardrail

The AI writes prose — never a number

The AI Model can shape
  • The CIO commentary and how it reads
  • Which considerations to weigh, from the documents & instructions
  • The emphasis and ordering of the narrative
The AI Model never invents
  • A figure — every number comes from the FACTS block
  • Suitability thresholds or the rebalance
  • Holdings — every dollar stays derived from the real book

Qualitative claims drawn from the client's documents are context — not independently verified. Every figure is computed deterministically by the engine.
